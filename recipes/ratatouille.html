Ratatouille

Description

A classic French vegetable stew with tomatoes, eggplant, zucchini, bell peppers, and onions.


Recipe

  • Eggplant
  • Zucchini
  • Bell peppers (red, yellow, and green)
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh herbs (basil, thyme, rosemary)
  • Salt and pepper

Procedure

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cut eggplant, zucchini, bell peppers, and onion into large dice.
  3. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil until softened.
  4. Add eggplant, zucchini, and bell peppers to the pan and cook until slightly softened.
  5. Transfer vegetables to a baking dish.
  6. Make tomato sauce by blending tomatoes, herbs, salt, and pepper.
  7. Pour tomato sauce over the vegetables.
  8. Bake for 45-60 minutes, or until vegetables are tender.
