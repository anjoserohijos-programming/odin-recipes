Arancini

Description

Crispy rice balls filled with risotto, meat, and cheese.


Recipe

  • Arborio rice
  • Chicken or beef broth
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Peas
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Egg
  • Flour
  • Olive oil

Procedure

  1. Cook arborio rice in chicken or beef broth until creamy.
  2. Stir in onion, garlic, butter, Parmesan cheese, peas, and cooked meat (optional).
  3. Let the mixture cool completely.
  4. Shape the rice mixture into balls and fill with mozzarella cheese.
  5. Coat each arancini in flour, then egg, and finally breadcrumbs.
  6. Deep fry or bake the arancini until golden brown and crispy.
