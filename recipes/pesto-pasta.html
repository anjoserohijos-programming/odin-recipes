Pesto Pasta

Description

A quick and flavorful pasta dish with fresh basil pesto.


Recipe

  • Dried pasta
  • Fresh basil
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Pine nuts
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Procedure

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions.
  2. While pasta is cooking, make pesto by combining basil, Parmesan cheese, pine nuts, garlic, olive oil, salt, and pepper in a food processor or blender.
  3. Drain pasta and toss with pesto.
  4. Serve immediately with additional Parmesan cheese if desired.
