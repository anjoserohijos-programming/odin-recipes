Lasagna

Description

A classic Italian layered pasta dish with meat sauce, ricotta cheese, and mozzarella.


Recipe

  • Marinara sauce
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Italian seasoning
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Salt and pepper

Procedure

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions.
  3. Brown ground beef and drain excess fat.
  4. Combine ground beef with marinara sauce, Italian seasoning, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  5. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a baking dish.
  6. Layer lasagna noodles, ricotta cheese, and mozzarella cheese.
  7. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of mozzarella cheese.
  8. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
  9. Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes.
  10. Remove foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until golden brown and bubbly.
